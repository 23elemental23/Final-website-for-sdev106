Blender art showcase!

This is a website deticated to being a an atr showcase from many CGI projects of mine. It showcases lots of 3d moddles, shaders, textures, backgrounds, lighting and other things. Most of it is made in blender, much of it mainly useing in godot engine or other projects.

I spend a lot of time maintly just moveing around coorinates on an xyz plane, as I make lots of triangles and faces. Ill often rotate them around, scale them to different sizes, duplicate them, or use any other tools to get the shapes right.

Theres a lot more to it. I spend time shading things, messing with textures and uv maps as well as moddifiers and lighting. Personally I find it fun mainly because it allows for one topic I can infinantly dive into and keep learning more and more about with no end or ceiling.
